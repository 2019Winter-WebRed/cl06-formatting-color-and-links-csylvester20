Does Music Help You Focus?

Pros and Cons of Listening to Music

In my opinion, music can help me focus but can also distract me. According to Mackenzie McCall "I don't like doing my work in silence but certain types of music can distract me". Studies show that you are more likely to focus on your work when the music doesn't have lyrics in it. When you are listening to a song with lyrics in it you want to sing along causing you to delay your work.

Classical, nature, white noise and more are the types of music that help you focus the most due to the calming nature of them. Types of music that are bad to listen to when trying to focus are rap, pop, and country. Some pros and cons of listening to music is that (1) it can help you focus but can also be a huge destraction and (2) when you listen to music you focus on the lyrics not the work that needs to be done so you can procrastinate while listening to music.
